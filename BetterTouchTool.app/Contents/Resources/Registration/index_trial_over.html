FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  • Is there any guarantee that BetterTouchTool will continue to work on upcoming OS X versions?

    No. There may always be changes in OS X that could make it impossible for BTT or some of its features to work. I hope this won't happen, but I (and you) can only be sure that BTT works on the currently released versions of OS X (as of April 2017).

  • Can I get a free license?

    If you think you have a good reason to request a free license, please contact me via boastr.net@gmail.com

  • Will the version I buy expire?

    No, starting with Version 1.6 BetterTouchTool won't expire anymore if a valid license is installed. I may however disable licenses at any time if they are illegally distributed/shared.

  • Can I use BetterTouchTool on multiple computers?

    Yes, if you buy a personal BetterTouchTool license you can use the license on all Macs you work with. It doesn't matter if it's your private Mac or your company's Mac. You can also install BetterTouchTool on two additional computers owned by family members of yours where you are not the main user.

  • Will updates be free forever? Or will there be paid updates?

    I guarantee that for at least 1.5 years after your purchase all updates will be free.


    Also your BetterTouchTool version will never expire if a valid license is installed. So the worst thing that could happen, is that you won’t receive free updates anymore after those 1.5 years, you’ll still be able to use your last version.

  • Will I get an invoice?

    Yes, I'm using Fastspring.com as payment/sales provider. They'll send you an appropriate invoice after your purchase.

  • Why don't you sell BetterTouchTool via the Mac App Store?

    Because it can not be sandboxed, thus Apple wouldn’t allow it on the Mac App Store.

    Please see https://www.boastr.net/btt-licenses/
